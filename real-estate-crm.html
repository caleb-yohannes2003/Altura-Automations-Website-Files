FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?

Most agents are fully live within 3–5 business days. We handle onboarding and training.

How will it qualify my leads?

It tracks responses from your funnels and forms, scores leads based on behavior, then sends personalized emails and texts to move them through your pipeline — from cold to booked.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. Our CRM is optimized for use on phones and tablets.

Do I need technical experience to use it?

Nope. It’s designed to be intuitive, and we offer full support along the way.

Can I migrate contacts from another CRM?

Yes — we assist with data migration from platforms like Follow Up Boss, Salesforce, and more.
