What Is Altura AI?

Altura AI is your fully managed automation assistant — built to handle client messages, missed calls, and appointment bookings for your business, 24/7. We use the power of artificial intelligence to help you grow, streamline, and professionalize your customer communication process.

What Is AI, and How Can It Help You?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) allows software to mimic human understanding, decision-making, and communication. At Altura, we harness conversational AI to simulate a real human assistant that understands your services, handles client messages, and manages your calendar — automatically.

Whether a client texts, or calls, our AI assistant can greet them, answer questions, gather service info, and book appointments — all without you needing to lift a finger. You’ll never miss a lead again.

How It Works

  • 1. Missed Calls → AI Response: Your AI assistant texts back missed callers instantly with a branded, helpful message.
  • 2. SMS & DMs → AI Replies: AI handles your inbound texts and calls, 24/7.
  • 3. Conversation Flow: The AI asks questions just like a real person would — What are you looking for? When are you available? — and guides them through the process.
  • 4. Appointments Booked: Once a lead is qualified, they’re booked into your calendar via Calendly. You get notified — they get a confirmation instantly.
  • 5. Logs Everything: You’ll receive a Google Sheet log of every conversation, sorted by platform, timestamp, and client contact.

The Onboarding Process

  1. Onboarding Form: You’ll fill out a Google Form with info about your services, pricing, availability, and business details.
  2. AI Training: We use your answers to teach your AI how to speak just like someone on your team.
  3. SMS Setup: We assign you a dedicated Twilio number (area code of your choice) to handle texts and calls.
  4. Testing + Demo: Once built, we send you a live demo and let you test your AI before going live.
  5. Launch + Access: You receive your AI assistant, a CRM tracker, and an update form to keep everything current.

Who This Helps

  • Contractors & Trades: Roofing, landscaping, concrete, painting — the AI handles the quotes, messages, and books the jobs while you're on-site.
  • Home Services: Cleaning, pest control, HVAC, junk removal — AI answers common questions and filters serious leads.
  • Beauty & Wellness: Hair salons, med spas, massage — AI can send pricing, book appointments, and follow up after sessions.
  • Anyone with Inbound Messages: If you receive DMs, calls, or texts from potential clients, this AI will save you time and convert more leads.

Why Choose Altura Automations?

  • Done-for-You Setup: No tech skills needed — we handle everything, start to finish.
  • Update Anytime: Promotions, price changes, vacation hours? Just send a request — your AI updates in 48 hours.
  • Clear Reporting: All conversations are logged, so you can review and follow up anytime.
  • Always Improving: We constantly improve and adjust your AI as your business grows.
